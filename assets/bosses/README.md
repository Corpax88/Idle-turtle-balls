Boss PNG assets
===============

Supported formats:

1. One combined boss sheet:
   assets/bosses/boss_sheet.png
   Layout: 5 columns x 2 rows, ordered left-to-right, top-to-bottom:
   1 Gear Goblin, 2 Brass Baron, 3 Steam Witch, 4 Cog Cyclops, 5 Boiler Brute,
   6 Rust Reaper, 7 Clockwork Queen, 8 Volt Vandal, 9 Pipe Phantom, 10 Chrono Warden.

2. Individual boss files:
   assets/bosses/00-gear-goblin.png
   assets/bosses/01-brass-baron.png
   assets/bosses/02-steam-witch.png
   assets/bosses/03-cog-cyclops.png
   assets/bosses/04-boiler-brute.png
   assets/bosses/05-rust-reaper.png
   assets/bosses/06-clockwork-queen.png
   assets/bosses/07-volt-vandal.png
   assets/bosses/08-pipe-phantom.png
   assets/bosses/09-chrono-warden.png

If both exist, individual files win. Missing assets fall back to the canvas-drawn boss.
